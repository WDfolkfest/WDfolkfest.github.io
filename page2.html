Festival Timeline

18 August 2023

Scandinavian Folk Music

On August 18 you can listen to 13 Scandinavian singers and their bands, including the well known Anna Sorensen, Mati Nilsen, and Lara Ellingboe. You can also enjoy Scandinavian snacks prepared especially for you, and if you are feeling brave you can practice sword fighting and archery.

19 August 2023

Balkan Folk Music

On August 19 you can enjoy 11 Balkan singers, among which are the much loved sister duo Alina and Maria Banich. While listening to the music we have prepared for you, you can continue attending the sword fighting and archery training sessions, and try out different foods common in the Balkan countries. 

20 August 2023

Final Concerts

The last day is reserved for folk artists from all over the world, there are 14 bands performing on all three stages available. The festival will end in the evening with a bonfire and guitar lessons from some of your favourite artists. Available places are limited for the guitar lessons, so please make sure to sign up for this on the first day of the festival!
