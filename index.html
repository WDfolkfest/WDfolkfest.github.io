About Folk Fest

Folk Fest is at its 13th edition this year and it is being held in the capital of Norway. Prior to this, it was organised in England twice, in Manchester and London, once in Ireland, France, Germany, Austria and Romania, to name a few countries where it found the most popularity. 

Over 350 artists from all over the world found a place on our stages, and over 45% of people who came to our festival returned for a second and a third experience. 

Not only do we support artists, but also locals who can organise fairs around the time of the fest, which can be planned to match the themes of our festivals.
